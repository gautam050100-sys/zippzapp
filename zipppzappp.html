Trip Cost Calculator

How to use calculator

  • Car owner is also counted as passanger.
  • Petrol cost get shared with the number of passangers.
  • Road toll get shared with the number of passangers.
  • Vehicle Maintenance Cost is 0.5rs/km.
  • Luggage cost is a seperate cost and got added, if applied.(Luggage upto 10kgs have no charge, beyond 10kgs 20rs on every kgs of weight).
  • *If the destination is not same , calculate seperate cost with 3.80rs/km.
